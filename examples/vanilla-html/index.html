About SightEdit

SightEdit is a revolutionary visual editing system that makes content management simple and intuitive. With our powerful yet lightweight library, you can transform any static website into a dynamic, editable experience.

Whether you're building a blog, e-commerce site, or corporate website, SightEdit adapts to your needs without imposing any specific structure or framework requirements.

  • Open source and free to use
  • Extensive plugin ecosystem
  • Enterprise-ready with advanced features
  • Active community and support
